About Statisfy


The goal of Statisfy is to better help you understand your music habits through the easy access to your Spotify Statistics. We use the Spotify Web API to display your recent listening history, top songs, albums, genres and many more statistics!

Your Data

Data we collect

  • We will collect public data from the Spotify application such as username, profile picture, users the account follows, followers, public playlists.
  • On top of the public information about the account, we will collect information about the user’s listening habits, recently played songs, playlists and artists, through Spotify’s API.
  • All data provided will be used for analysis in order to create relevant and personalized statistics.
  • In addition, the user will have to give permission from the Spotify app before our app will be able to collect any data.

How we use your data

  • All user data will be stored in our database.
  • The data will only be accessible by us (our team of developers) and the user
  • We will only keep the user data as long as is needed to provide the service. The user will be able to request data deletion at any time.
  • The privacy policy explains the exact use and storage of data collected through Spotify.

Privacy Policy

The information that we collect is used for providing and improving the service. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this policy.
